About Dyscalculia Support

What is Dyscalculia?

Dyscalculia is a learning disability that affects the ability to understand numbers and learn math facts. Our platform provides visual and interactive tools to help overcome these challenges through step-by-step problem solving, visual representations, and adaptive learning.

How We Help

  • Visual math problems with objects and diagrams
  • Step-by-step problem breakdown
  • Number line visualization
  • Adaptive difficulty based on performance
  • Progress tracking and skill assessment
