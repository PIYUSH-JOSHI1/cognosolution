About Dysgraphia Support

What is Dysgraphia?

Dysgraphia is a learning disability that affects writing abilities. It can impact handwriting, spelling, and the ability to put thoughts on paper. Our platform provides tools to help improve writing skills through guided practice, analysis, and structured exercises.

How We Help

  • Writing analysis for common mistakes
  • Letter formation guidance
  • Structured writing prompts
  • Progress tracking for improvement
  • Spelling and grammar assistance
