About Dyslexia Support

What is Dyslexia?

Dyslexia is a learning disability that affects reading fluency and comprehension. Our platform provides specialized tools to help overcome these challenges through AI-powered text simplification, phonics training, and personalized learning experiences.

How We Help

  • Text simplification that preserves meaning
  • Adjustable fonts and spacing for better readability
  • Text-to-speech with synchronized highlighting
  • Phonics games for sound-letter mapping
  • Progress tracking and personalized recommendations
