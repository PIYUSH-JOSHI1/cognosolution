About Dyspraxia Support

What is Dyspraxia?

Dyspraxia is a developmental coordination disorder that affects movement and coordination. Our platform provides interactive exercises and games to help improve motor skills, balance, and coordination through structured practice and real-time feedback.

How We Help

  • Balance and stability exercises
  • Coordination training games
  • Live camera movement tracking
  • Progress monitoring and feedback
  • Adaptive difficulty levels
